About Awash Insurance

Founded in 1994, Awash Insurance was launched following the liberalisation of the financial sector in Ethiopia making it one of the pioneer insurance companies in the country. Awash Insurance stands on a solid foundation with its richly unique and diverse composition which speaks to our ability to understand customer needs.
